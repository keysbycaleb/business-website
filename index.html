FAQ

Common questions answered. Still curious? Just ask.

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on scope, but most projects range from 2-8 weeks. A simple landing page might take a week, while a full mobile app could take 2-3 months. I'll give you a realistic timeline during our discovery call.

Do you work with existing codebases?

Absolutely. Whether you need to enhance an existing application, fix bugs, or add new features to legacy code, I'm comfortable diving into established projects.

What if I need changes after launch?

All projects include a revision period. For ongoing changes, I offer maintenance retainers starting at $149/month, or you can request changes on an hourly basis.

How does pricing work?

I offer both project-based pricing and hourly rates ($75/hr). Most clients prefer project-based quotes for predictable budgeting. Every quote is custom—you'll never pay for features you don't need.

What technologies do you specialize in?

My core stack is React, React Native, TypeScript, Next.js, and Firebase/Supabase. I focus on these tools because they're powerful, well-supported, and let me build high-quality applications efficiently.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. For larger projects, I typically structure payments as 50% upfront and 50% on completion. For very large projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments.
