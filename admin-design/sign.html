SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT AGREEMENT

Mobile Application Development Services

This Software Development Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of the Effective Date by and between:

Provider: Lanting Digital LLC, a California limited liability company ("Provider")

Client: iAttend LLC ("Client")

1. SCOPE OF WORK

Provider agrees to develop a mobile application ("App") for Client according to the specifications outlined in this Agreement. The App will be a cross-platform React Native application providing event check-in and attendance tracking functionality.

2. DELIVERABLES

The following deliverables are included in this Agreement:

  • iOS and Android mobile application
  • Admin dashboard web interface
  • Backend API and database setup
  • User documentation
  • 30 days of post-launch support

3. PROJECT TIMELINE

Milestone Deliverable Target Date
1 UI/UX Design Approval January 15, 2026
2 Core Features Development February 15, 2026
3 Beta Testing March 1, 2026
4 Final Delivery & Launch March 15, 2026

4. PAYMENT TERMS

Total project cost: $8,500.00

Payment Amount Due
Deposit (upon signing) $2,550.00 (30%) Upon Signing
Milestone 2 Payment $2,975.00 (35%) Feb 15, 2026
Final Payment $2,975.00 (35%) Upon Delivery

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Upon full payment, all intellectual property rights in the deliverables shall transfer to Client, except for any pre-existing Provider IP which shall be licensed to Client on a non-exclusive, perpetual basis.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to keep confidential any proprietary information disclosed during the course of this Agreement and shall not disclose such information to third parties without prior written consent.

7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

8. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this Agreement with 14 days written notice. Upon termination, Client shall pay for all work completed to date and Provider shall deliver all completed work product.
